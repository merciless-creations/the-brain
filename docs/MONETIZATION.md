# Monetization Strategy - The Brain

## Business Model Overview

**The Brain** is a **subscription-based SaaS platform** for non-fiction authors. Revenue comes from tiered monthly/annual subscriptions with usage-based pricing for AI features.

## Payment Processing

### Payment Providers

**Stripe** (Primary)
- Credit/debit cards
- ACH transfers
- International payments
- Invoice generation
- Subscription management
- Webhook automation

**PayPal** (Secondary)
- Alternative for users without credit cards
- International reach

### Billing Cycle

- **Monthly**: Billed on signup date each month
- **Annual**: Billed upfront (17% discount)
- **Grace period**: 3 days after failed payment before suspension
- **Prorated upgrades**: Credit applied immediately
- **Downgrade timing**: Takes effect at end of billing cycle

### Refund Policy

- **30-day money-back guarantee** (no questions asked)
- Pro-rated refunds for annual plans (if <6 months used)
- No refunds on add-ons after delivery

## Churn Reduction Strategy

### Why Non-Fiction Authors Churn

1. **Book is finished** - Natural completion point
2. **Slow progress** - Lose momentum
3. **Price sensitivity** - Budget constraints
4. **Feature gaps** - Missing critical functionality

### Retention Tactics

#### 1. Progress-Based Retention
- Weekly "progress report" emails
- Milestone celebrations (50%, 75%, 100% done)
- "What's next?" prompts after book completion
- Template for next book type

#### 2. Value Reminders
- Monthly newsletter: "Author success stories"
- Feature spotlight emails
- Usage stats: "You've written X words this month"
- AI token savings calculator: "Saved $X in editing costs"

#### 3. Pause Option
**"Hibernate Mode"** - $5/month
- Keep all data
- No AI features
- Read-only access
- Resume anytime at full price

Reduces hard churn by 30-40%.

#### 4. Exit Surveys
Automated when canceling:
- "What made you consider leaving?"
- Offer discount (20% off 3 months)
- Offer downgrade to Free tier
- Offer pause option
